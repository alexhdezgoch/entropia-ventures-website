Infrastructure Fixes

Actionable Protocol

These structural changes are the highest-leverage path to page 1. Most can be implemented within 30 days.

A

Fix the homepage title tag immediately — "Cleburne Attorney | Family Law & Criminal Defense | Robert E. Luttrell III"

This is the single highest-ROI fix on the site. A descriptive title tag containing the city, practice areas, and attorney name can produce first-page movement within 30 days at zero content cost.

B

Build separate landing pages for family law and criminal defense in Cleburne

The site currently mixes practice areas. Splitting them into dedicated pages with Johnson County geo language would allow the firm to rank for both verticals independently instead of competing against itself.

C

Leverage JAG background in bio and on practice pages as a trust signal

The Air Force JAG story — recognized as one of the top trial attorneys in the Air Force — is a credibility anchor competitors in Cleburne cannot match. It belongs prominently on the criminal defense page and homepage.
