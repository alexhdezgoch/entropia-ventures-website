Infrastructure Fixes

Actionable Protocol

These structural changes are the highest-leverage path to page 1. Most can be implemented within 30 days.

A

Diagnose and fix indexation — Google Search Console submission and noindex audit

Zero indexed pages is a technical emergency, not a content problem. The immediate priority is determining whether robots.txt, a noindex directive, or a crawl block is preventing indexation — then resolving it so Google can discover the site's content at all.

B

Claim Google Business Profile and set up at 515 E Las Olas Blvd

A Las Olas Boulevard address is a credibility signal in Fort Lauderdale. A complete GBP with photos, practice areas, and reviews can surface in the local map pack in 2–4 weeks — faster than organic rankings — and differentiate Cohen Legal Offices from the other Cohen firms in the market.

C

Build separate Personal Injury and Family Law practice pages with LegalService schema

Two distinct practice areas require two distinct pages to rank for two distinct client intents. Schema on each page confirms the practice area to Google's crawlers and opens the path to rich-result eligibility once the site is indexed.
